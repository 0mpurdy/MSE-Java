Alfred John Gardiner was born in November 1884. He lived in London and was local at the Streatham meeting.

Mr. Gardiner was a trustee of the Stow Hill Depot for nearly 20 years, November 30, 1948 – August 8, 1967. He accepted responsibilty with the other trustees (Wm. Henderson, G. H. Stuart Price and Robert Stott) for the 1962 revision of the hymn book.

The Lord took Mr. Gardiner to Himself c. 1976 (precise date unknown).
