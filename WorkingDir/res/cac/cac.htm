Charles Andrew Coates was born on December 7, 1862, in Bradford, Yorkshire, but for the greater part of his life he lived in Teignmouth, Devon, on the south coast of England. He was soundly converted in 1878 at the age of 16. Mr. Coates suffered from physical weakness for a good part of his life. He fell asleep at 9:20 am on Lord's Day, October 7, 1945.
